# Day 28 – Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator

## 📌 Project Overview

Today I built an interactive **Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator** using **Claude AI**, HTML, Tailwind CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript. The simulator demonstrates how hospitals coordinate patient admissions by validating documentation, insurance, prior authorization (PA), physician orders, consent, and bed availability before approving admission.

The application provides a realistic healthcare operations workflow where the user plays the role of a **Hospital Admission Coordinator** and makes decisions to improve patient admission readiness.

---

## 1. Admission Readiness Dashboard

- Displays initial readiness score.
- Shows workflow checklist.
- Tracks documentation, insurance, consent, PA, physician orders, and bed assignment.
- Displays risk indicators.
- Interactive workflow actions.


## 2. Final Admission Decision

- Final Readiness Score
- Admission Summary
- Checklist Status
- Final Admit / Not Ready decision
- Restart Case option

# 🎯 Key Learnings

- Hospital admissions require coordination across multiple departments.
- Prior Authorization significantly affects admission readiness.
- Insurance verification is critical before patient admission.
- Complete documentation reduces denial risk.
- Physician orders and patient consent are mandatory before admission.
- Bed management directly impacts operational efficiency.
- Healthcare teams collaborate throughout the admission process.
- Governance metrics help monitor operational performance.
- Risk management improves patient safety and workflow efficiency.
- Interactive simulations simplify understanding of complex healthcare operations.
