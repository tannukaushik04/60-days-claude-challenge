What this tool does

This is a personal system for writing better prompts for Claude and ChatGPT, built around how you actually work as a developer. It doesn't store one-off prompts — it stores reusable workflows (prompt templates with fill-in variables) and building blocks you snap together, so you spend less time re-explaining context and re-editing AI output.
