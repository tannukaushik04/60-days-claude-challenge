# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## 🚀 Project Overview

Today I built **Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab**, an interactive enterprise simulation that teaches supply chain crisis management through realistic business scenarios. The application places the user in the role of a Supply Chain Leader who must respond to operational disruptions, negotiate with suppliers, make executive decisions, invest in AI technologies, and evaluate business performance through an executive dashboard.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Understand supply chain crisis management.
- Practice enterprise decision-making.
- Learn supplier negotiation strategies.
- Explore AI applications in supply chain operations.
- Improve business continuity planning.
- Analyze operational KPIs through interactive dashboards.

---

# 🛠 Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- React (CDN)
- Babel JSX

# 💡 Personalized Feedback

### Biggest Mistake

Accepted a supplier price increase too early, resulting in reduced cost efficiency during negotiations.

### Best Decision

Activated the backup supplier quickly, minimizing production downtime and improving customer service.

### Expert Recommendation

Develop a standardized crisis response playbook and strengthen supplier diversification to improve future resilience.

### Lessons Learned

- Backup suppliers are essential during disruptions.
- Customer communication is critical in maintaining trust.
- AI significantly improves operational decision-making.
- Balanced decisions outperform purely cost-focused strategies.
- Risk monitoring enables faster response to unexpected events.
