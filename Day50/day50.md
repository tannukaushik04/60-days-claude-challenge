# Day 50 – Defend Your Experience

## 📌 Project Overview

Today, I built **Defend Your Experience**, an AI-powered adaptive interview preparation platform that helps users confidently defend every claim made in their resume, portfolio, LinkedIn profile, research, or professional experience.

Instead of simply reviewing a resume, the application intelligently extracts meaningful claims and transforms them into personalized interview challenges. It simulates realistic interview scenarios by asking adaptive follow-up questions, identifying weak evidence, testing storytelling ability, and providing actionable feedback through a comprehensive Defense Report.

Built as a **single offline HTML application** using **HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript**, the platform delivers a premium interview rehearsal experience with interactive progress tracking, confidence indicators, claim validation, session history, local storage, exports, and a modern responsive UI inspired by commercial interview coaching platforms.

This project demonstrated how AI can create personalized interview simulations that improve confidence, communication skills, and the ability to defend real-world experiences under pressure.
