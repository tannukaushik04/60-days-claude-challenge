# Day 16 – Custom Skills for Stock Fundamental Research

## Objective

Learn how Claude Custom Skills can be used to create reusable workflows for stock fundamental analysis and generate detailed research reports without repeatedly entering long prompts.

---

# Custom Skill Created

**Skill Name:** stock-fundamental-research

### Purpose

A reusable workflow that performs structured stock research including:

* Business overview
* Financial analysis
* Valuation analysis
* Ownership trends
* Growth assessment
* Risk analysis
* Investment thesis
* Peer comparison

---

# Stock Selected for Testing

**Company:** Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

### Report Generated

TCS Deep Dive Research Dashboard

The report included:

* Company overview
* Financial health analysis
* Revenue and profit growth trends
* Valuation metrics
* Ownership insights
* Risk assessment
* Investment outlook

# Key Findings from TCS Analysis

## Strengths

* Strong business quality
* Debt-free balance sheet
* Consistent dividend payments
* Strong cash generation
* High ROE and ROCE

## Risks

* AI-driven pricing pressure
* Slowing IT spending globally
* Revenue growth moderation
* Legal exposure risks

## Valuation Observation

* Trading near historical valuation lows
* Dividend yield remains attractive
* Fair-to-cheap relative to historical averages

# Comparison Capability

The same skill can be reused for:

* Infosys
* Reliance Industries
* HDFC Bank
* Tata Motors
* ICICI Bank
* Other NSE/BSE listed companies

No need to re-enter the entire workflow prompt.

---

# Benefits of Custom Skills

1. Saves time.
2. Creates standardized analysis.
3. Produces consistent reports.
4. Reduces prompt repetition.
5. Improves research quality.

---

# Key Learnings

* Custom Skills convert long prompts into reusable workflows.
* Stock analysis becomes faster and more consistent.
* Reports can be generated for multiple companies using the same framework.
* Reusability significantly improves productivity.
* Structured workflows reduce the chance of missing important financial metrics.

---

## Conclusion

Custom Skills enable reusable stock research workflows. By creating the stock-fundamental-research skill, detailed fundamental reports can be generated repeatedly for different companies without rewriting prompts, improving both productivity and consistency.
