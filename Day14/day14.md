# Recommendation

## Apply with Caution

The internship description itself is reasonable for freshers.

However, the company has a very limited digital presence despite being established several years ago.

Before applying:

- Verify MCA/ROC registration
- Search for official website
- Check client portfolio
- Look for employee reviews
- Verify LinkedIn company page

If verification is successful, the opportunity may still be useful for gaining experience.

---

# Smart Interview Questions

### Question 1
Can you walk me through a real project I would contribute to and explain the technology stack involved?

### Question 2
How many interns have been hired previously and what career outcomes did they achieve?

### Question 3
How is mentorship structured for interns?

### Question 4
What determines whether the internship lasts 1 month or 3 months?

### Question 5
Can you share examples of live products or client projects delivered by the company?

# Key Learnings

1. Job applications should be evaluated just as carefully as candidates are evaluated.
2. Company credibility is an important factor before accepting an internship.
3. AI can identify hidden red flags that may not be obvious initially.
4. Asking intelligent interview questions helps validate opportunities.
5. Remote jobs should always be independently verified.
6. Risk analysis can prevent wasting time on low-quality opportunities.
7. Professional due diligence is a critical career skill.
