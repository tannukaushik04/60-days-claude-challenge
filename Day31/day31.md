# Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower

## Challenge

Build an **AI Supply Chain Control Tower** using a **single HTML file** with **HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript**.

The application simulates the role of a **Head of Operations** responsible for monitoring live supply chain disruptions, prioritizing operational incidents, making corrective decisions, and maximizing business performance before time runs out.

---

# Project Overview

The simulator recreates a modern enterprise **Supply Chain Control Tower** where operational alerts continuously appear.

As the Head of Operations, the player must quickly evaluate incidents, choose the best response, and maintain business KPIs while managing multiple disruptions simultaneously.

The game concludes with an executive performance dashboard summarizing operational effectiveness.

---

# Features

- Premium dark enterprise dashboard
- Live operational alerts
- Random supply chain disruptions
- Priority-based incident management
- Multiple active alerts
- Live KPI monitoring
- Countdown timer
- Event log
- Animated KPI cards
- Critical alert pulse animation
- Hover effects
- Pause functionality
- Sound toggle (visual)
- Help / Instructions modal
- Responsive desktop & mobile layout
- Final executive performance dashboard
- Play Again functionality

---

# Operational KPIs

The simulator continuously updates:

- Service Level
- Customer Satisfaction
- Inventory Health
- Transportation Efficiency
- Operating Cost
- Revenue Protected
- Score
- Remaining Time

---

# Operational Alerts

Random alerts generated during gameplay include:

- 🚨 Port Congestion
- 🚨 Supplier Delay
- 🚨 Truck Breakdown
- 🚨 Warehouse Running Out of Stock
- 🚨 Customs Inspection
- 🚨 Demand Spike
- 🚨 Factory Machine Failure
- 🚨 Weather Disruption
- 🚨 Wrong Inventory Count
- 🚨 Damaged Shipment

Each alert displays:

- Alert Title
- Description
- Priority Level
- Remaining Time
- Business Impact

---

# Available Actions

Each operational alert provides corrective actions such as:

- Expedite Shipment
- Use Backup Supplier
- Reroute Trucks
- Increase Production
- Transfer Inventory
- Approve Air Freight
- Ignore
- Delay Decision

Each action dynamically affects business KPIs and the overall operational score.

# Key Learnings

During this project I learned:

- How Supply Chain Control Towers monitor enterprise operations in real time.
- Why prioritizing high-impact incidents is critical for business continuity.
- How operational decisions directly influence customer satisfaction, inventory, transportation, revenue, and cost.
- The importance of balancing multiple KPIs instead of optimizing only one.
- How dashboards help operations leaders monitor live business performance.
- How JavaScript can create dynamic simulations with timers, random events, animations, and interactive decision-making.
- How responsive dashboard design improves usability across devices.
