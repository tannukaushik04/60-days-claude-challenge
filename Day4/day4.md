# Day 4 – Chain-of-Thought Prompting & Career Roadmap

## Topic

Chain-of-Thought Prompting

## What I Learned

Chain-of-Thought Prompting improves AI output by encouraging the model to reason through a problem step-by-step before generating a final answer.

### Key Learnings

1. Better reasoning

   * Breaking complex problems into smaller steps improves accuracy.

2. More reliable outputs

   * AI evaluates assumptions before providing recommendations.

3. Personalized solutions

   * Responses become tailored to the user's situation and goals.

4. Real-world applications

   * Useful for career planning, business strategy, project planning, and decision-making.

---

# Prompt Used

You are an Elite AI Career Strategist.

Your goal is to build a personalized roadmap for me.

Before creating the roadmap, ask me ONLY these 4 questions:

1. What is your current situation?
2. What skills do you currently have?
3. What is your target goal?
4. What is your target timeline?

After collecting all answers:

Think step by step.

1. Analyze my current position.
2. Identify strengths.
3. Identify skill gaps.
4. Identify the fastest path to the goal.
5. Recommend learning priorities.
6. Recommend projects.
7. Recommend networking strategy.
8. Create milestones.

Finally generate a visually structured ONE-PAGE roadmap.
