📝 Summary

The leadership team reviewed Q2 performance, which came in at 12% revenue growth against an 18% target. Key gaps were driven by enterprise deal slippage, lower-than-expected conversion rates, and a known reporting performance issue affecting both existing customers and prospects. The team aligned on deferring several decisions — including hiring and conference budget — until further data is available, and flagged engineering capacity as a key constraint.

💡 Key Takeaways

Q2 revenue grew 12%, falling short of the 18% annual target. 5 enterprise deals (of 14 expected) slipped into next quarter due to procurement delays and security review requests.
Reporting performance is a cross-functional priority — it's driving support ticket increases and coming up during sales calls with prospects.
Engineering is near capacity; adding website work may require contractors. No cost estimate yet.
Hiring decisions deferred until Q3 forecasts are finalized. Conference budget deferred until vendor proposals are received.
August analytics dashboard release date is not yet guaranteed by the CTO.
