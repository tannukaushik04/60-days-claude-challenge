# Day 5 – Context Engineering

## What is Context Engineering?

Context Engineering is the practice of providing relevant information, constraints, goals, background, and personal details before asking AI to perform a task.

The quality of context often matters more than the prompt itself.

### Key Benefits

1. Personalized Outputs
2. Higher Accuracy
3. Better Planning
4. Better AI Systems and Agents

# Key Learnings

* Context dramatically improves AI output quality.
* Personalized prompts generate more actionable plans.
* AI makes fewer assumptions when context is provided.
* Context Engineering is a foundational skill for building AI agents.
* The same prompt can produce completely different results depending on the context supplied.


---

# Conclusion

This experiment demonstrated that Context Engineering is one of the most powerful AI skills. The roadmap generated with context was far more personalized, practical, and aligned with my goal of building a career in Data Science and AI.
